Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Master Chef Li Wei, Dragon Palace brings authentic Chinese cuisine with generations of culinary tradition.

Using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods, we create dishes that honor China's rich culinary heritage while embracing modern techniques.
